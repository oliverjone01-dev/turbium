Increase visual impact and personality in designs that are too safe, generic, or visually underwhelming.

## MANDATORY PREPARATION

Use the frontend-design skill -- it contains design principles, anti-patterns, and the **Context Gathering Protocol**. Follow the protocol before proceeding -- if no design context exists yet, you MUST run teach-impeccable first.

---

## Assess Current State

1. **Identify weakness sources**: Generic choices, timid scale, low contrast, static, predictable, flat hierarchy
2. **Understand the context**: Brand personality, purpose, audience, constraints

**CRITICAL**: "Bolder" doesn't mean chaotic or garish. It means distinctive, memorable, and confident.

**WARNING - AI SLOP TRAP**: When making things "bolder," AI defaults to: cyan/purple gradients, glassmorphism, neon accents on dark backgrounds, gradient text on metrics. These are the OPPOSITE of bold -- they're generic. Bold means distinctive, not "more effects."

## Amplify the Design

### Typography Amplification
- Replace generic fonts with distinctive choices
- Extreme scale: 3x-5x size jumps, not 1.5x
- Weight contrast: Pair 900 with 200, not 600 with 400
- Variable fonts, display fonts, condensed/extended widths

### Color Intensification
- Increase saturation to 70-85% (but not neon)
- Bold, unexpected color combinations -- avoid purple-blue gradient AI slop
- Dominant color strategy: one bold color owns 60% of the design
- Tinted neutrals that harmonize with your palette

### Spatial Drama
- Extreme scale jumps: 3-5x larger for important elements
- Break the grid: let hero elements escape containers
- Asymmetric layouts with tension
- Generous white space (100-200px gaps)
- Intentional overlap for depth

### Visual Effects
- Dramatic shadows for elevation (not generic drop shadows)
- Texture & depth: grain, halftone, duotone -- NOT glassmorphism
- Custom elements, illustrative details that reinforce brand

### Motion & Animation
- Entrance choreography with 50-100ms stagger delays
- Scroll effects: parallax, reveal animations
- Satisfying hover effects and click feedback
- Smooth transitions using ease-out-quart/quint/expo

**NEVER**:
- Add effects randomly without purpose
- Sacrifice readability for aesthetics
- Make everything bold (then nothing is bold)
- Ignore accessibility
- Copy trendy aesthetics blindly

## Verify Quality

- **NOT AI slop**: Does this look like every other AI-generated "bold" design? If yes, start over.
- **Still functional**: Can users accomplish tasks?
- **Coherent**: Does everything feel intentional and unified?
- **Memorable**: Will users remember this experience?
